Miedzywodzie (before: Heidebrink) it's an adorable and small (about 500 inhabitants) village. It's surrounded by forest. Village features clean water and wide beach.

If you fancy fishing, there is "Kamienski Lagoon" on the South. Even if you don't enjoy fishing, a walk to lagoon is recommended - because of its beauty. Many German pre-war postcards shows "Kamienski Lagoon". Lagoon has got its name because of the huge stone sticking out of the water (kamien = stone).

Miedzywodzie is a good place for cycling/hiking. If you are interested in golf, you should visit nearby Lukecin. Miezdywodzie is blooming during the summer.
